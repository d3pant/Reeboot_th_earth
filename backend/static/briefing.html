Evacuation Go-Bag Checklist

Pack these now so you can leave within 15 minutes if a critical threat is declared.

Personal

  • Government ID, passport, driver's license
  • Insurance cards (health, property, crop, livestock)
  • Cash and credit/debit cards
  • Phone and charger (car charger, power bank)
  • Prescriptions and 7-day medication supply
  • Eyeglasses or hearing aids

Documents

  • Property deeds and mortgage papers
  • Livestock registration and vaccination records
  • Crop insurance policies and recent receipts
  • Tax records (last 3 years)
  • Birth certificates, social security cards
  • Vehicle titles and registration
  • USB drive or cloud backup of farm records

Animals

  • Halters, leads, and transport crates
  • Portable water containers and 3-day feed
  • Veterinary records and medications
  • Photos of each animal (proof of ownership)
  • Pet carriers and leashes

Farm Records

  • Backup of farm management software
  • Field maps and irrigation schedules
  • Equipment inventory and serial numbers
  • Contact list: vets, suppliers, neighbors

Vehicle Preparation

  • Fuel tank filled (do this NOW)
  • Tire pressure and spare checked
  • Emergency kit: water, food, first-aid (3 days)
  • Blankets, change of clothes, sturdy shoes
  • Flashlight and extra batteries

Don't forget

  • Family photos and irreplaceable items
  • Comfort items for children
  • Evacuation contact addresses
  • Paper maps of evacuation routes
